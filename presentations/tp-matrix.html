Teaching Presentation Grading Matrix

Objective 6 - 25 points

  • 5 points - Written materials in the presentation are grammatically correct and error free.
  • 5 points - Verbal communication (in person or in video) is clear and focused on the topic.
  • 5 points - The presentation is well paced and fits within the allowed time of 7 minutes.
  • 5 points - The presentation provides accurate, thorough information (Why, When, Where, How) about the topic.
  • 5 points - The presentation contains a working example with an accompanying explanation such that the example can be reproduced and used by the audience.
